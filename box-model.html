The Amazon Shopping app is a feature-packed mobile application that allows users to browse, search, and shop from millions of products with ease. It offers advanced tools like visual search, barcode scanning, and voice commands, making product discovery quick and intuitive. Augmented reality features, such as “View in Your Room” and 3D previews, help shoppers visualize products before purchasing. Powered by AI, the app delivers personalized recommendations, shopping guides, and tailored alerts for sales and new arrivals. With secure sign-in options, real-time order tracking, flexible delivery choices, and 24/7 customer support.


flipkart logo


The Flipkart app is a popular Indian e-commerce platform offering a wide range of products, from groceries to electronics, through a simple and user-friendly interface. It features tools like image search, a grocery tab, and a premium Brand Mall Mode for a cleaner shopping experience.


meesho logo


Meesho is a social commerce app from India that lets users shop affordably and earn by reselling products through platforms like WhatsApp and Instagram. It offers fashion, home goods, and more at wholesale prices with free shipping, cash on delivery, and easy returns. The lightweight app supports small sellers by enabling them to start online stores with zero investment, making it one of the fastest-growing shopping platforms in the world.
